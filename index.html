OUR STORY

Building Dreams With Precision & Passion

Buffalo Construction is a diverse provider of professional technical services for a variety of industrial/commercial projects. Our core strategy is building long term client and employee relationships through implementing values such as loyalty, honesty, cost benefit and follow through. This allows us to attract and retain only the most qualified crafts, thus providing superior customer value. This strategy yields cost advantages for our clients by performing project duties correctly the first time and eliminating project rework.

From large-scale industrial facilities to modern commercial spaces and custom dream homes, we blend traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge construction techniques. Our reputation is built on quality, reliability, and the lasting relationships we form with our clients.
